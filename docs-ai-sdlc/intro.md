# AI SDLC

**Part I is in progress.** This course covers the AI-assisted software development
lifecycle: how to plan, build, review, test, and ship software when AI agents are
part of the team — with the same first-principles, "why it breaks" rigour as the
[RAG & Agents course](/rag-agents/).

This page is a placeholder while the first lessons are written. Real content lands
here soon; the structure and URLs are stable, so links you save now will keep
working.

## What this course will cover

- **Planning and specification** with AI in the loop — what to hand an agent and what to keep.
- **Building and reviewing** — agent-assisted implementation, and the review discipline that keeps it honest.
- **Evaluation and guardrails** for the development process itself, not just the product.

Check back as Part I ships. In the meantime, the
[RAG & Agents course](/rag-agents/) is complete and available in English, Russian,
and Slovak.
